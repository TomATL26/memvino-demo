## About VinoVerse

VinoVerse is an innovative product that combines physical wine cork keepsakes with digital memory preservation. Our QR-enabled decorative thumbtacks turn ordinary wine corks into interactive memory capsules.

### Product Concept

- **Physical Product**: Elegant thumbtack with embedded QR code that inserts into wine corks
- **Digital App**: Free mobile app to capture and store photos, videos, notes, and wine information
- **Use Cases**: Special occasions, wine collecting, restaurant experiences, wedding favors

### Business Model

- Free app with freemium subscription ($4.99/mo after 15 memories)
- QR device bundles (B2C): Starter ($24.99), Collector ($59.99), Universe packs
- B2B partnerships with fine dining restaurants
- Integration with Vivino and CellarTracker

### Market Opportunity

- $10.14B wine accessories market (5.2% CAGR)
- Growing experiential dining trend
- Wedding favor and personalized keepsake market
